Directions

The workshop will take place in the Ecology Building (Ekologihuset) at Lund University.

How to get to Lund?

You can find all the useful information on how to get to Lund here.

In brief: the nearest major airport is in Copenhagen (Kastrup). From the airport you can take the train (Öresunds tåg) towards Sweden (Malmö), which continues on to Lund C. The trains run every 20 min or so, and tickets can be purchased from the kiosks in the main intrance to the airport ticketing area.

How to get to Ekologihuset?

The Ecology Building is located up near the Astronomy building (with the historical astronomy tower) and the LTH tram stop. From the main square at Lund C, it is around a 15-20 minute walk, or a 7 min tram or bus ride. The closest bus stops are LTH (for the tram) or Ridhuset (if taking a Green bus).

Note: public transit in Lund is cash-free(!) you can buy a ticket from (i) one of the ticket machines at Lund C; (ii) by blipping a credit card when getting on the tram or green city busses; or (iii) using the Skånetrafiken app.

The best route to the Ecology Building will depend on where in town you are staying, but you shouldn't go too far astray using Google Maps.
